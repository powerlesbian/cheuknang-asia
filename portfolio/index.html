Cheuk Nang Lookout

Victoria Peak, an iconic landmark in Hong Kong, offers a breathtaking panorama that encapsulates the city’s beauty. From its summit, visitors are treated to a stunning view of the skyline, with towering skyscrapers, the bustling harbor dotted with boats, and the lush greenery of the surrounding hills. The Lookout, a historically significant area, provides a serene escape from the city’s hustle and bustle, making it a perfect spot for both owners and visitors to enjoy the natural and urban splendor of Hong Kong.

Location: No. 30 Severn Road, The Peak, Hong Kong · Setting: a landmark Peak address with panoramic city and harbour views
